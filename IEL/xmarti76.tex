$U_{XY} = \left|{R_1 \cdot \frac{U}{R_1 + R_4} - R_2 \cdot \frac{U}{R_2 + R_5}}\right| =
\left|{\frac{315 \cdot 180}{315 + 460} - \frac{615 \cdot 180}{615 + 300}}\right|
